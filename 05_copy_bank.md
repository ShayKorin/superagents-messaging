## Naming & Language Conventions

| Use This | Not This | Why |
|----------|----------|-----|
| "Superagent" | "bot", "chatbot", "AI assistant" | Positions it correctly — proactive, not reactive |
| "handles", "manages", "runs" | "helps you", "assists with" | Active verbs = more powerful |
| "tell it what to do" | "configure", "set up", "program" | Human, not technical |
| "always on" | "runs in the background" | More visceral, more reassuring |
| "ready in under a minute" | "quick setup", "easy to use" | Specific and believable |
| "Base44 Superagents" | just "Superagents" (in brand contexts) | Brand attribution matters |
