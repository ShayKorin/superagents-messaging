## Language Rules

| ✅ Use | ❌ Avoid | Why |
|--------|---------|-----|
| "handles", "runs", "acts", "monitors" | "helps you", "assists with" | Active = more powerful |
| "tell it what to do" | "configure", "program", "set up" | Human, not technical |
| "always on" | "runs in the background" | More visceral |
| "under a minute" | "quick", "fast", "easy to set up" | Specific = believable |
| "proactive" | "automated" | Automation = rules; proactive = intelligence |
| "Superagent" | "bot", "chatbot", "AI assistant" | Positions correctly |
| "Base44 Superagents" | Just "Superagents" (in brand contexts) | Brand attribution |
| "describe what you want" | "prompt it" | Human framing vs. technical |
